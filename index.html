Protections
BUILT FOR
SAFETY

Complete anonymity

Your identity is never recorded. We use zero-knowledge architecture so that even we cannot determine who submitted a report.

  • No IP logging or browser fingerprinting
  • No account or login required
  • Tor-compatible submission process

End-to-end encryption

Every report is encrypted the moment you submit it. Only authorized reviewers with the decryption key can read your submission.

  • 256-bit AES encryption standard
  • Encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Keys rotated automatically

Evidence support

Attach documents, images, and files to substantiate your report. All attachments are encrypted with the same standards.

  • Support for documents, images, and audio
  • Metadata automatically stripped from files
  • Secure file storage with expiration

Legal protection

Whistleblower protection laws shield you from retaliation. We provide guidance on your rights and the legal frameworks that protect you.

  • Compliant with EU Whistleblower Directive
  • Guidance on national protection statutes
  • Connection to legal support resources

YOUR
PROTECTIONS
01

Anti-retaliation law

National and international laws protect you from dismissal, demotion, or any negative consequence for reporting in good faith. We help you understand your rights.

02

EU Whistleblower Directive

The 2019 EU Directive mandates confidential reporting channels and strict anti-retaliation measures across all member states. Organisations must respond within 3 months.

03

Legal support access

We connect you with pro-bono legal resources and support organisations if you face retaliation or need guidance on the investigation process.
